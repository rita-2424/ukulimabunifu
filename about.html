How FarmerLink Works

Connecting smallholder farmers directly with buyers to eliminate middlemen, reduce post-harvest losses, and ensure fair prices for fresh produce.

The Problem We Solve

Smallholder farmers across Kenya face a critical challenge: after harvest, they struggle to find buyers quickly. This leads to:

Post-Harvest Losses — Produce spoils before reaching a buyer

Exploitative Middlemen — Forced to sell at suppressed prices to traders

Low Farm-Gate Prices — Earnings don't reflect true produce value

Limited Market Access — No way to reach potential bulk buyers

FarmerLink provides a digital solution: Direct farmer-to-buyer connection with zero commission, real-time order tracking, and no intermediaries.
